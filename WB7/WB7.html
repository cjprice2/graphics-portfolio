Graphics Projects

3D Aircrafts
3D aircrafts and radars made from primitives and lathes.
  • Built with Three.js and the CS559 Framework.
  • Aircrafts assembled from cylinders and boxes.
  • Radars assembled from spheres, cylinders, and lathes.
  • Ground plane, ambient light, and directional lighting.
  • Camera controls: left-mouse drag = orbit, right-mouse drag = pan, scroll = zoom.
3D Construction Site
3D construction site with articulated motion made from primitives extruded geometry.
  • Built with Three.js and the CS559 Framework.
  • Construction site assembled from cylinders and boxes and some extruded geometry.
  • Use left mouse to rotate the camera, use right mouse to move the camera, and use the scroll wheel to zoom in and out.
  • Use the sliders to experiment with the articulated motion!
