3D Buildings with Textures
3D buildings with textures made from primitives.
  • Built with Three.js and the CS559 Framework.
  • Pyramid hip house and open gable house made from primitives.
  • Textures loaded and mapped from files.
  • Camera controls: left-mouse drag = orbit, right-mouse drag = pan, scroll = zoom.
  • Roof texture from here: https://pngtree.com/so/roof-texture -- other textures given in class.
